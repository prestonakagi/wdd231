Salt Lake Shred

Utah's UFA professional team is the Salt Lake Shred. Their fourth season is in 2025. "The team's mission is to lift the quality of ultimate that is played at all levels in the state of Utah." They do this by making development programs and help with high school and professional players. The team loves Ultimate Frisbee, hard work, and sportsmanship. They have won one West Division championship and are striving to get another.
