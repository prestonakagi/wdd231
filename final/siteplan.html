WDD231 Final Project Site Plan

Site Name and Logo

I chose the name "Ultimate Frisbee: an Upcoming Sport", because because Ultimate Frisbee is a relatively new sport and keeps developing and gaining popularity.
Logo: an Ultimate Frisbee

Site Purpose

Introduction to Ultimate Frisbee mainly to someone who doesn't know the sport. This will include explaining the rules and that there is a professional league and professional teams.

Scenarios

“Scenarios are questions asked by a site visitor who represents the organization's target audience. Who is target audience?”
Target audience is college undergraduate students (and maybe high school students) and curious biochemistry or molecular biology liking adults.
At least 2 scenarios :
What is Ultimate Frisbee?
How do you play Ultimate Frisbee?
Is there professional Ultimate Frisbee?
Why should I care about Ultimate Frisbee?
