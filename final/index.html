Intro to Ultimate Frisbee

Ultimate Frisbee is a sport mainly known and played in the United States. According to the World Flying Disc Federation, Ultimate Frisbee was invented in 1968 in Maplewood, New Jersey by some Columbia High School students. It is a sport with goals and mechanics similar to soccer and American football. This sport is a great way to keep fit (from all the running), learn teamwork, develop good sportsmanship, and have fun!

To learn how to play Ultimate Frisbee, please click the "How to Play" in the top navigation.

Ultimate Frisbee Association (UFA)

The Ultimate Frisbee Association (UFA) started in 2012. There are 24 teams in the UFA. The association works to show and popularize Ultimate Frisbee. UFA streams game live on their platform, watchUFA.tv, and has clips of games, merchandise, rules, and more on their website, watchufa.com.
